My research is in **computational social choice**, **AI & governance**, and **decentralized systems**.
I study how groups of agents can make collective decisions in a principled, algorithmically tractable way,
and how these ideas apply to emerging institutions such as DAOs, participatory budgeting platforms,
and AI-mediated governance.
