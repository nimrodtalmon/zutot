### Thinking about joining?

I welcome inquiries from motivated students interested in **computational social choice**,
**AI & governance**, or **decentralized systems**. Before reaching out, please:

- Read a couple of recent papers from the [research](/zutot/research/) page to get a feel for the work.
- Send a short email with your background, what interests you about the research, and what you hope to work on.
