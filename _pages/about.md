> *"You Are What You Is"* &mdash; Frank Zappa

I am a faculty member in the Department of Computer Science at Ben-Gurion University of the Negev (BGU).
My research sits at the intersection of computer science, economics, and social organization: I work on
**computational social choice**, **AI &amp; governance**, and **decentralized systems** (blockchain, DAOs, trust networks).
I am also a home-recording musician.
